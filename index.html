About Me

I am currently a Software Engineer at Meta working on Privacy Aware Infrastructure for GenAI. My work focuses on infrastructure that helps product and platform teams build AI systems with privacy controls, data-flow visibility, and operational guardrails built into the development process.

This site is a public home for my work at the intersection of privacy, artificial intelligence, backend infrastructure, and developer experience.

Endeavor

My long-term focus is privacy infrastructure for artificial intelligence: the systems, tools, and engineering patterns that make it possible to understand, constrain, and verify how sensitive data moves through AI products.

As GenAI systems become more capable and more deeply integrated into consumer and enterprise products, privacy work needs to move from policy documents and one-off reviews into reusable infrastructure. That means better data lineage, policy enforcement, asset classification, developer workflows, and runtime controls that can scale with product velocity.
